Dalton Newbend is an artist from [Dexter, GA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dexter,_Georgia). They received their BFA in Studio Art and minor in Art History from [Columbus State University](https://columbusstate.edu/art/) in 2020. They were also a 2019 fellow at [Mildred’s Lane](https://mildredslane.org/).
