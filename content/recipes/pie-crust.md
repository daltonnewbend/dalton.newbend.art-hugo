#### ingredients
	300 g    flour
	13 g     sugar
	5 g      sea salt
	1 cup    cold butter
	~ 1 cup  ice water


#### instructions
- mix dry ingredients, kneed in butter
- slowly add in water
- roll out to 1/4" thick. save leftover for top
- bake at 400 for 15 min
- pull from oven, add pie crust weights, bake another 10-15 min
