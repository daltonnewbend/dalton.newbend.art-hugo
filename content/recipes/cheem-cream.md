# cheem cream

#### ingredients
	226g    softened cream cheese
	2 tbs   softened butter
	100g    powdered sugar
	1 tsp   vanilla
	1 tsp   black pepper


#### instructions
- mix ingredients together thoroughly

#### notes
great on [oatmeal cream pies](/oatmeal-cream-pies/) or [[shortbread cookies]]
